    4 Cheese Ravioli!

    Savor the richness of our Four Cheese Ravioli, where tender pasta pillows embrace a decadent blend of ricotta, Parmesan, mozzarella, and Asiago cheeses. Each bite delivers a symphony of creamy textures and robust flavors, perfectly complemented by a delicate garlic butter sauce that enhances every mouthful. Dive into a dish that embodies comfort and sophistication, exclusively crafted to tantalize your taste buds.

    Lobster Rolls!

    Experience the coastal delight of our Lobster Rolls, featuring succulent chunks of tender lobster meat nestled in a toasted buttery bun. Each bite is a celebration of freshness, enhanced by a light dressing of lemon aioli and a sprinkle of chives, delivering a taste of seaside indulgence that promises to satisfy.

    Tuscan Grilled Chicken!

    Enjoy our Tuscan Grilled Chicken, a culinary journey inspired by Italy's rustic flavors. Tender chicken breast marinated in a blend of Tuscan herbs and spices, grilled to perfection, and served with a medley of roasted vegetables. This dish captures the essence of Tuscany with every savory bite, offering a satisfying and wholesome dining experience.
